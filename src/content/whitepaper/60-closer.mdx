The work behind these inquiries is a practice, not a report. It starts by making the network legible — who holds context, where data moves, where it stalls. It maps the relationships where meaning gets made or lost. And it designs the structures, roles, and rhythms that let an organization act on what it's making sense of. We'll share more of the method as the case studies that test it come in.

We're in ongoing conversations with leaders working this problem in energy, philanthropy, and AI adoption. If you recognized your organization somewhere in these pages, we'd like to talk.
